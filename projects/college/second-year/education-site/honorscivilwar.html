Honors Civil War

Honors U.S. Civil War students study the U. S. Civil War period in-depth. The political, social, cultural, and economic climates of the time period are explored. Decisions of individual leaders and the impact on soldiers and civilians are explored. Causes and consequences - both short and long term - are examined. Military campaigns, tactics and strategies are analyzed. The honors level provides expectations and opportunities for students to work independently, at an accelerated pace, to engage in more rigorous and complex content, and to develop projects and products that reflect that level of understanding. Instruction in honors classes is at a rigorous pre-Advanced Placement level. Students will be expected to complete challenging assignments above grade level, both in and out of class.
